What is ez wraps?

Fast, Accurate and versatile

EZ Wraps offers a new and innovative way to create wrapping cutouts for boxes. Simply input the dimensions of your box, or upload an SVG file, and EZ Wraps does the rest!

  • Get Started

Streamlining design processes

EZ Wraps allows you to automate the process of making wrapping for your boxes. No longer will you have to open your box net file and manually create flaps and teeth so that your wrapping fits perfectly. EZ Wraps automatically generates flaps and teeth cutouts to ensure that your box is perfectly wrapped every time - eliminating the need for trial and error and material wastage. EZ Wraps also gives an SVG file as an output, allowing a seamless transition to machining/cutting tools.
